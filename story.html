Our story

A café made

Bread & Bean is a family-owned café dedicated to making everyone feel welcome—and very well fed.

Whether you are grabbing a coffee first thing, catching up over a leisurely brunch, meeting for a tentative first date or simply eating excellent cake, this is a place to settle in.

Our food is homemade using fresh ingredients, free-range eggs and produce from suppliers we trust. The atmosphere is unfussy, friendly and unmistakably local.
